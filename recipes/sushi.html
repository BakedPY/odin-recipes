Spicy Ahi Roll

Ingridients

  • Nori Sheets
  • Sushi Rice
  • Cucumber
  • Ahi(Raw Tuna)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Soy Sauce & Wasabi(optional)

Description

A sushi favorite for many across the globe. Delicous, healthy, simple, the motto for most of my favorite recipes. Make this for guest and you're sure to not dissapoint. Now let's get to it!

Steps

  1. Cook rice then set aside to cool down to room temp.
  2. Cut cucumber into french fries shapes. Don't forget to remove the seeds(soft middle part of cucumber).
  3. Slice Ahi into french fry sizes as well.
  4. Lay Nori on a flat surface and spread a thin layer of rice evenly on the Nori.
  5. Add your Ahi, Cucmber, mayonnaise, and wasabi if you choose, into a row, on one of the neds of the Nori.
  6. Roll tight like if you were rolling a cigar.
  7. Slice into bite size pieces or keep as a handroll.
  8. Gently dip into some soy sauce and enjoy your creation.
