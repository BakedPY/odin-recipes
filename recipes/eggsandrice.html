Eggs & Rice

Ingridients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Rice(Even great with day old rice)
  • Green onions
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Description

A staple breakfast during my childhood and still my go to meal when I want something quick and delicous. This is a simple eggs and rice but you can spice it up with anything you desire. Scramble it all together and you got fried rice, sort of...

Steps

  1. Cook rice if you don't have any left over rice.
  2. Heat a frying pan on medium heat with some olive oil
  3. Cook eggs sunny side up or scrambled
  4. Cut your green onions into small pieces
  5. Plate and enjoy!
