Veggie Lasagna

Ingridients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Zucchini
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Description

This is a simple but delicous lasagna recipe that anyone can make. It's healthy and great for non-meat eaters and the comfy aromas filling your kitchen will send smiles to everyone around. Now lets get to it!

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees celcius.
  2. Cut Zucchini into thin slices and set aside.
  3. Cook pasta sheets in boiling water until "al dente", then drain and set aside.
  4. Layer base of baking pan with a little olive oil and tomato sauce.
  5. Cover the base with a layer of pasta sheets you set aside. Then add a layer of slice zucchini on top the layered pasta sheets. Sprinkle salt and pepper over layer.
  6. Repeat this process starting with another layer of tomato sauce, etc. until you have at least four layers
  7. Add a top layer of mozzarella cheese, covering the layers below.
  8. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes or until the cheese is golden brown.
  9. Now just portion it up and enjoy!
